## 🔍 Why Emails Go to Spam Initially

### Common Reasons:
1. **New Sender**: Gmail doesn't recognize `corvetheq@gmail.com` as a bulk sender yet
2. **Automated**: Gmail detects automated sending patterns
3. **No Sender Reputation**: Your email account hasn't built trust yet
4. **HTML-Heavy**: Rich HTML emails sometimes trigger filters

### What Gmail Checks:
- ✅ Do recipients open emails?
- ✅ Do recipients reply?
- ✅ Do recipients mark as spam?
- ✅ Is the sender verified?
- ✅ Does content look like spam?
